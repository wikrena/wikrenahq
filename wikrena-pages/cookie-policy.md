# Cookie Policy

		Effective Date: December 27, 2025  ·  Last Updated: March 9, 2026

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Wikrena uses them on wikrena.com, and how you can manage your cookie preferences. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

We use cookies to improve your experience, understand how our site is used, and support our marketing efforts — always in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

					Section 01

## What Are Cookies

		Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website when you visit it. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and deliver a more personalized experience.

Cookies can be session cookies (deleted when you close your browser) or persistent cookies (stored on your device for a set period). They can be set by us (first-party) or by third-party services we use.

					Section 02

## How Wikrena Uses Cookies

		We use cookies to:

- Keep you logged in and remember your preferences
- Understand how visitors interact with our website and services
- Improve our website's performance and content
- Deliver relevant marketing and retargeting content
- Prevent fraud and ensure security
					Section 03

					Section 04

## Third-Party Cookies

		Some cookies on our site are set by trusted third-party services we integrate with, including:

- Google Analytics — website analytics and performance tracking
- Google Tag Manager — tag management
- Meta Pixel — marketing and retargeting (with consent)
- Payment processors — fraud prevention and transaction security
- Embedded content providers (e.g., YouTube, Vimeo) — may set their own cookiesWe do not control third-party cookies. Please review their respective privacy and cookie policies for details.

					Section 05

## Managing Your Cookie Preferences

		You have several options to manage or disable cookies:

- Browser Settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies via their settings menu
- Our Cookie Banner: When you first visit wikrena.com, you can accept or decline non-essential cookies
- Opt-Out Tools: Use Google Analytics Opt-out (tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout) to prevent analytics tracking
- Industry Tools: Visit youronlinechoices.eu or optout.aboutads.info for ad cookie opt-outsPlease note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality and experience of our website.

					Section 06

## Cookie Retention Periods

		Cookie lifespans vary by type:

- Session cookies — deleted when you close your browser
- Analytics cookies — typically 13 months
- Functional preference cookies — up to 12 months
- Marketing cookies — up to 90 daysWe review and update our cookie list regularly to ensure it remains accurate.
