		OUR STORY

## How It Started.

		Wikrena was born from lived experience. In 2015, our founder began his career in retail, first as a storekeeper, then as a branch sales manager in Enugu. It was there he discovered how deeply data shapes decisions, growth, and change in business.

While selling for his aunt and managing day-to-day operations, the lesson became undeniable: data is not just numbers, it is the backbone of better decisions.

During his undergraduate studies, he saw how impactful research depends on the proper use of data. These experiences sparked a conviction that would become Wikrena's founding purpose.

“Data is not just numbers, it is the backbone of better decisions.”

— Chris Awoke, Founder

					2015

The Spark

Working in retail in Enugu, our founder saw firsthand how data drives business decisions. The seed of Wikrena was planted.

2018

The Problem Became Clear

During undergraduate studies, he noticed the lack of practical data guidance available to African students and businesses alike. A gap he resolved to fill.

2021

Wise Breed Analytics Founded

The company launched with a mission to make data more practical, accessible, and transformative — starting with training and analytics services.

2023

Training at Scale

Over 137 students trained in Data Analytics. Corporate training programs launched. Partnerships with businesses to help them make sense of their data.

2025

Wikrena — A New Chapter

Rebranded as Wikrena with an expanded vision: become a major data, AI, and analytics infrastructure company for Africa. Three pillars. One mission.

NOW →

Building for Africa

Deeper into analytics services, education, and digital workplace transformation. The gap between data and impact is closing.
