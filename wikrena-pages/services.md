## How a Wikrena Engagement Works.

		Every engagement follows the same four steps regardless of which service you choose. No scope creep. No invoice you did not expect.

  1

#### Discovery Call

    We spend 30 minutes on a call understanding your business, your data situation, and what you are trying to solve. This is not a sales call. It is a diagnostic. At the end we tell you honestly whether we can help and which service fits your situation. If we are not the right fit, we say so.

  2

#### Proposal and Scope

    Within 48 hours of the discovery call we send you a written proposal. It includes the exact scope of work, what we will deliver, the timeline, and the investment. No vague estimates. No hidden fees. You know exactly what you are agreeing to before you sign anything.

  3

#### Engagement & Delivery

###### QUESTIONS

## Frequently Asked
Questions.

		Have a question not answered here? Email hello@wikrena.com - we respond within 24 hours.

												How much do your services cost?
					Every engagement is scoped individually because every business situation is different. We do not publish fixed prices because a one-hour advisory session and a three-month data project are very different things. Book a discovery call and we will give you a clear, honest proposal after understanding your needs.

												Do we need to have clean, organised data before working with you?
					No. Most of our clients come to us with messy, scattered data across spreadsheets, CRM tools, and manual records. Data cleaning and preparation is part of what we do. Bring us what you have.

												How long does a typical engagement take?
					A Data Strategy engagement typically takes two to three weeks. A Data Analysis and Reporting project is usually three to six weeks depending on data complexity. Corporate Training is designed around your team&#8217;s availability. We give you a clear timeline in your proposal before work begins.

												Do you work with businesses outside Nigeria?
					Yes. Our clients are primarily in Nigeria but we work remotely across Africa. If you are building a business on the continent, we want to work with you.

												What tools do you use?
					We work primarily with Power BI, Excel, SQL, and Python for analysis and reporting. We match our tools to what your team can actually maintain after we are done. There is no point building something complex that breaks when we leave.

												Can we start small before committing to a larger engagement?
					Yes. Many clients start with a Data Strategy session to understand what they need before committing to a larger project. That is a completely reasonable way to start and we actively encourage it.
